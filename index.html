How to survive

WASD — move   double-tap W — sprint (15s of stamina)   Space — jump
Mouse drag — look   1–9 — hold a hotbar item (or click it in the inventory)
E — interact: search, pick up, read
I — inventory   F — eat food   T — wait one hour   H — this panel

Follow the objective line (top right): the wreck, the map, the cabin,
and what waits on the great hill will lead you out. Or not.

Staying alive: eating heals you (F) — berries grow on bushes, grubs hide in
rotten stumps, and rations are packed away in luggage and crates.
Health also mends slowly on its own. Stamina refills when you stop sprinting.

Danger: bears hold their territory and will charge; wolves hunt at night;
snakes bite if you stand over them. All of them fear the fire — but don't stand IN it.

Stay near the fire at night. It does not like the fire.
It will not cross running water. Nobody remembers why we know that.
